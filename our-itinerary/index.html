Sam and Dave’s adventures in France

Our itinerary

3-5 July : to Annecy [to pick up the tandem]

9 - 14 July : 6-day ride in the Jura

16 - 22 July : 6-day ride in the Luberon

23/24 July : trip to the Pont du Gard [so Sam can reminisce about her childhood holidays]

25-28 July : cycle from Agen to St Foy-le-Grande

28 July - 4 August : Buddhist retreat at Plum Village (near Bordeaux)

4 -8 August : cycling the beaches on the Atlantic coast (near Arcachon)

9 - 17 August : 9-day ride in the Dordogne (the Périgord)

18 - 22 August :cycle across the Massif Central (Cahors via Puy-en-Velay to Retournac)

22 - 29 August :visit our friends Renée and Dédé on their goat farm in Retournac

30 August : back to Lyon

13 September : move back to the UK!

Notes:

  1. we’re now re-thinking whether we want to do the Luberon ride after too many hills in the Jura.
  2. most of the routes are taken from the Lonely Planet Cycling France guide if you want more details.
